About

Less is more work

We are a Student's Club here at NMIT, Bengaluru.
We provides a platform for students to learn and grow in the field of UI/UX.

Our Club has focus on designing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, hosting workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects to enhance skills in user research, prototyping, and design tools like Figma and Adobe XD.
